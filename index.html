Hello! I'm Caitlin Marshall, front-end developer.

About Me

I’m a front-end web developer based in Portland, Maine. I love turning great design into the real deal: responsive, fast, and dependable websites. Skilled in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React and WordPress, I write code that is concise and collaboration-ready. I'm also diving into Python and SQL, learning how to use data to tell powerful stories.

I’m following my passion for coding to pursue my other big passion, helping solve the climate crisis. Active with my local chapter of 350.org, I’ve done outreach to help stop an oil pipeline project through South Portland, started a climate action group for young families, and built out a database of municipal climate actions across Maine. My goal is to build a career at the intersection of computers and climate solutions.

When I’m not clacking away at my computer, I love to have my hands in the dirt! And sometimes I'll even grow something edible.
