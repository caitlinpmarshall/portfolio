About Me

I’m a web developer and designer based in Portland, Maine. Getting technical, that means I use the languages of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with tools including Adobe Xd, Photoshop and WordPress, to design and build responsive, mobile first sites. Or in other words, I can make you a pretty website that’s nice to use, by anyone, on any device.

I’m following my passion for coding to pursue my other big passion, helping solve the climate crisis. Active with my local chapter of 350.org, I’ve done outreach to help stop an oil pipeline project through South Portland, started a climate action group for young families, and built out a database of municipal climate action across Maine. My goal is to build a career at the intersection of computers and climate solutions.

When I’m not clacking away at my computer, I love to have my hands in the dirt! And sometimes I'll even grow something edible.
