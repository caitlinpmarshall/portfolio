About Me

I’m a web developer from Portland, Maine. I’ve always thought computers were pretty darn cool, and while my career path has traveled through elementary education, local foods retail, and solar energy, I’m so excited to return to this early love. I use HTML and CSS to craft websites that are thoughtful, friendly, and maybe even a little delightful. As a student at Skillcrush, I’ve got my eye on honing my skills in Python to create powerful data visualizations. When I’m not clacking away at my computer, I love to have my hands in the dirt! And sometimes I'll even grow something edible.
